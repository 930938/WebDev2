This is banjo, the loveable bear and bird. These two started their adventures all the way back, their first game came out in june 29th of 1998. Their first game was a huge succsess, selling nerly 2 million copies. Everybody loved the game and wondered what could come next? The next game shocked everybody, a game called banjo-tooie. This was basically the first game but a million times bigger with interactive worlds and a ton more levels. The next and final main game was nuts and bolts. Instead of platforming you ride a car and complete challanges. Fans were outraged about this change, "This isnt the banjo game we wanted" After a while fans started liking the game, its not a true banjo game but appernetly it is an epic game with a lot to offer. Overall Banjo has been though a lot from platformers to a racing game to a collecter but though it all Banjo has remained an amazing part of video game history.
